Press Angles: Why publications might cover DripEmails.org

1) The Inbox Automation Angle
- Headline concept: “This startup turns inbox conversations into automated drip follow-up.”
- Why it works: It reframes email automation from outbound-only to inbound-engagement.

2) The Productivity Angle
- Headline concept: “Teams lose revenue in follow-up gaps—DripEmails closes the loop.”
- Why it works: Strong pain point for sales, support, founders, and agencies.

3) The Practical AI Angle
- Headline concept: “AI writing that supports real email workflows, not just drafting.”
- Why it works: Shows practical AI utility in existing communication systems.

4) The SMB/Startup Angle
- Headline concept: “A lightweight way for small teams to run personalized follow-up at scale.”
- Why it works: Useful for budget-conscious teams without large RevOps stacks.

5) The Technical Builder Angle
- Headline concept: “Open, API-friendly email workflow platform built on Django/Python.”
- Why it works: Appeals to technical publications and developer audiences.

Proof points to include in outreach
- Gmail + IMAP integrations for auto-reply workflows
- Multi-step campaigns with wait-time sequencing
- Subscriber/list management + import/export
- AI generation/revision support
- Analytics dashboard and campaign tracking
- SMTP + API-friendly architecture

Suggested publication categories
- SaaS and startup media
- Marketing operations and growth newsletters
- Sales productivity and RevOps outlets
- Founder/operator communities
- Developer tooling and Python ecosystem publications

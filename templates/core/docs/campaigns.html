{% trans "Automatic Email Sequencing" %}

{% trans "How It Works" %}

DripEmails.org automatically schedules the next email in your campaign sequence whenever an email is sent. This means you can set up a complete drip campaign and it will run automatically without any manual intervention!

{% trans "Example Flow:" %}

  1. {% trans "Email 1" %} (order 0, wait 1 day) - Sent 1 day after subscription
  2. {% trans "Email 2" %} (order 1, wait 2 days) - Automatically scheduled 2 days after Email 1 is sent
  3. {% trans "Email 3" %} (order 2, wait 3 days) - Automatically scheduled 3 days after Email 2 is sent

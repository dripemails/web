Automatic Email Sequencing

How It Works

DripEmails.org automatically schedules the next email in your campaign sequence whenever an email is sent. This means you can set up a complete drip campaign and it will run automatically without any manual intervention!

Example Flow:

  1. Email 1 (order 0, wait 1 day) - Sent 1 day after subscription
  2. Email 2 (order 1, wait 2 days) - Automatically scheduled 2 days after Email 1 is sent
  3. Email 3 (order 2, wait 3 days) - Automatically scheduled 3 days after Email 2 is sent

ðŸ’¡ Key Points:

  • Each email's wait time determines when the next email will be sent
  • Emails are scheduled automatically when the previous email is successfully sent
  • All scheduled emails appear in "Recent Send Activity" on your dashboard
  • The system uses cron.py to process scheduled emails (no Celery required)
  • If you add a new email to a campaign that has already sent emails, it will automatically schedule to all active subscribers

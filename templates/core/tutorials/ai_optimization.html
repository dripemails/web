AI-Powered Email Improvement

Use AI to revise, optimize, and enhance your existing email content for better engagement and conversion rates.

üí° Pro Tip: AI optimization works best when you give it specific goals like "improve clarity" or "make it more persuasive".

What AI Can Optimize

  • Clarity: Make your message easier to understand
  • Conciseness: Reduce word count while keeping meaning
  • Tone: Adjust formality, urgency, or friendliness
  • Persuasiveness: Strengthen calls-to-action
  • Grammar & spelling: Fix errors and improve flow
  • Engagement: Make content more interesting

Using the AI Revision Feature

  1. Select the text you want to improve in the email editor
  2. Click the "Optimize with AI" button
  3. Choose an optimization type or enter a custom instruction
  4. Review the AI's suggested revision
  5. Accept, reject, or manually combine with your original

Optimization Types

Make it Shorter

Condense without losing key information. Perfect for mobile readers.

Make it More Professional

Elevate tone for B2B or formal communications.

Make it More Casual

Friendly, conversational tone for consumer brands.

Add Urgency

Create FOMO for time-sensitive offers.

Strengthen CTA

Make call-to-action more compelling.

Subject Line Optimization

Improve subject lines for higher open rates:

  1. Enter your current subject line
  2. Click "Optimize Subject Line"
  3. AI suggests 5 improved versions
  4. Each optimized for: clarity, curiosity, urgency, or benefit
  5. Choose the best or combine elements

A/B Testing with AI

Create variations for testing:

  1. Write your email as Version A
  2. Use AI to create a variation with different approach
  3. Test both versions with segment of your list
  4. Send winning version to remaining subscribers
  5. Learn which approach works best

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: AI suggestions are starting points. Always verify they match your brand voice and message accuracy.

Custom Optimization Prompts

Give AI specific instructions:

  • "Make this more benefit-focused, less feature-focused"
  • "Add more social proof and credibility"
  • "Simplify this for a non-technical audience"
  • "Make this sound more exciting and energetic"
  • "Remove jargon and use simpler language"

Optimization Workflow

  1. Draft: Write your email first (human creativity)
  2. AI Review: Let AI suggest improvements
  3. Selective Apply: Choose which suggestions to use
  4. Personal Touch: Add your unique voice back
  5. Final Review: Read through as a recipient would

Best Practices

  • Use AI as a second opinion, not the final word
  • Optimize subject lines for your specific audience
  • Test AI-optimized content against your original
  • Don't over-optimize ‚Äì maintain authenticity
  • Keep your brand voice even when using AI suggestions
  • Focus on one optimization goal at a time
  • Track performance to see if AI changes improve results

When to Use AI Optimization

  • Your emails are too long
  • Open rates are declining
  • CTAs aren't getting clicks
  • Tone doesn't match your brand
  • You need fresh variations for testing
  • Content feels stale or repetitive
